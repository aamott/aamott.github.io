Simple Robotic Car Backend

Simple Robotic Car Backend | GitHub

A robotic car control system built for the AI Society at BYU - Idaho. Meant to be used with very simple robot cars - the kind typically used for self-driving car projects. It supports two types: a "standard" car, with a servo for steering, and a "tank" car, which is controlled by changing the speed of motors on either side of the car.
